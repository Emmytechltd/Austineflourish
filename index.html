About Me

Austine Flourish is a versatile bassist and composer known for his expressive performances on both upright and electric bass. With a foundation in classical training and a passion for genres like jazz, funk, and soul, Austine crafts immersive musical experiences that resonate with diverse audiences. His musical journey began in Abuja, Nigeria, where early exposure to jazz music, acoustic music, and musical storytellers ignited his passion for music. Over the years, Austine has honed his skills, drawing inspiration from renowned bassists and integrating elements from various musical traditions into his unique style. Austine's performances are characterized by rhythmic precision, melodic depth, and a commitment to storytelling through music. Whether collaborating with ensembles or leading solo projects, he aims to create memorable moments that connect with listeners on a profound level. Currently, Austine is involved in collaboration with Thai Chi Kitchen Jazz night, Transcorp Music, Church Concert, continually pushing the boundaries of his artistry and exploring new musical landscapes.
